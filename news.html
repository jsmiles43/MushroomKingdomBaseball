Three-team trade marks first of the season

June 15, 2016

Less than five games before the game-15 trade deadline, the Boo Oogie Oogies, Bowser Monsters, and Mario Fireballs have agreed to a three-team trade that will send Wiggler to the Boo Oogie Oogies, Blue Kritter "Jerro" to the Bowser Monsters, and Baby DK to the Mario Fireballs.

The move was originally born from discussions between the Mario Fireballs and Bowser Monsters swapping Wiggler for Baby DK, but the Boo Oogie Oogies stepped in to offer Blue Kritter "Jerro" to the Monsters to secure Wiggler, creating a contingency for Baby DK to make his way to the Mario Fireballs.

Blue Kritter "Jerro" has a proven track record of power with 6 home runs and an OPS of .984 last season. This season, "Jerro" has platooned as a pitcher to limited success with 3.23 ERA, 1-2 record, and four home runs given up thus far. At the plate, "Jerro" has struggled (.094/.237/.281) but has two home runs. A move to the Boo Ooogie Oogies for "Jerro" means more at-bats, and hopefully a return to last year's slugging form. Because the Oogies' rotation is full of aces, "Jerro"'s involvement as a pitcher will either be limited to relief or cease altogether.

Though Baby DK is struggling (.227/.244/.273), Wiggler is looking to rename the Mendoza line with only one hit on the season (.037/.037/.074). Last season, Wiggler slashed .161/.204/.333 for the Waluigi Smart Alecks. Though Wiggler did bat clean-up most of the season on the Smart Alecks, he showed glimpses of pop with 4 home runs and 6 RBIs on a team whose OPS was just .486.

The Boo Oogie Oogies will hope to tap into Wiggler's long ball potential while firmly bolstering their fielding. Wiggler's chemistry with the three of the Oogies' players (Blooper, King Boo, and Petey Piranha) will make for a better infield because of the increase throwing strength that second baseman Blooper and shortstop Petey Piranha will exhibit.

With Baby DK, the Fireballs look to quadruple Wiggler's batting average and turn their two-win season around in the second half of the season.
